Information We Collect

This website does not currently collect personal information directly. We may use analytics cookies (with your consent) to understand site traffic and improve your experience. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

Cookies

We use cookies only with your explicit consent. When you visit our site, a cookie consent banner will appear allowing you to accept or deny non-essential cookies. Essential cookies required for basic site functionality do not require consent.

Analytics cookies: If accepted, we may use Google Analytics to collect anonymised usage data such as pages visited, time spent on site, and referring URLs. This data is used solely to improve the website experience. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

Local storage: We use browser local storage to remember your cookie consent preference and your chosen theme (light or dark mode). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Your Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have rights regarding your personal data including the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of your data. As we do not currently collect personal data beyond anonymised analytics, these rights are limited in scope on this site. When our services launch, this policy will be updated accordingly.
